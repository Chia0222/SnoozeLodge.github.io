About Us

" One of the leading student accommodation facilities of SNOOZE LODGE management is located in Kampar, Perak. ".

Our properties are strategically located in Bandar Baru Kampar, at the centre of Westlake Residential, where University Tunku Abdul Rahman (UTAR) is only 600m away; Tunku Abdul Rahman University College (TAR UC) and Westlake International School are within walking distance. Being adjacent to these higher learning institutions makes SNOOZE LODGE’s properties easily accessible to students. Other amenities such as banks, clinics, “mamak” /restaurants, hypermarkets, convenience stores, and recreation outlets are also within the vicinity. Outdoor recreation activities such as bird watching, trekking, fishing, caving and white water rafting are plentiful within easy reach from our accommodation..
