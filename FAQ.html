1. What are the available room options?
We offer a variety of room options, including single room, double rooms and whole units. Each room is fully furnished and equipped with amenities such as a desk, chair, bed, closet, and shared bathroom facilities.
2. Is parking available for students who rent a room?
Parking availability varies by location, but we do offer parking options for students who rent a room. Please contact us for more information about parking availability.
3. What is included in the rental fee?
The rental agreement may include additional fees such as utilities for electricity, water, and internet that the students are responsible for paying, in addition to the security deposit and first month's rent. These fees will be clearly outlined in the rental agreement.
4. How do I pay the rental fee and other associated costs?
Payment can be made online through our secure payment portal using a credit card or bank transfer. We also accept cash or check payments in person.
5. What is your cancellation policy?
Our cancellation policy varies depending on the length of the rental agreement. Generally, we require at least 30 days' notice for cancellation and may retain a portion of the security deposit.
6. Can I tour the rental rooms before making a reservation?
Yes, we offer tours of the rental rooms by appointment. Please contact us to schedule a tour.
7. Is parking available for students who rent a room?
Parking availability varies by location, but we do offer parking options for students who rent a room. Please contact us for more information about parking availability.
8. Is there a cleaning service provided for the rental rooms?
We do not provide regular cleaning services for the rental rooms. It is the responsibility of each student to keep their room and shared facilities clean and tidy.
9. Is there a security system in place for the rental building?
Yes, we have a security system in place, including surveillance cameras and secure access to the building. However, students are still responsible for taking precautions to ensure their safety, such as locking their doors and reporting any suspicious activity.
10. What happens if I damage something in the rental room?
Students are responsible for any damage they cause to the rental room or shared facilities. The cost of repairs or replacement will be deducted from the security deposit.
